Anarkali Magazine.

Anarkali Magazine was founded in 2024 and is run by a group of highly talented university students, all from a multitude of diverse backgrounds. The aim of the magazine is to provide a platform for aspiring authors, poets and artists from all over Pakistan. There are countless talented young minds across the nation that never get the chance to share their art with the world. We aim to change that.

In many cases, the major spotlight is awarded to those who have the privilege or the desire to create art in English. However, we believe that the individuals who aspire to gain success through writing in their native languages should receive the same or perhaps even more significant opportunities. In the hopes of perhaps aiding in the decolonisation of the literary culture in Pakistan, we decided to launch Anarkali. For now, the magazine is open to writers in English, Urdu and Sindhi and we hope to expand on the medium one day.
